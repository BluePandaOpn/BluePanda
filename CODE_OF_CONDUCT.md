# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

BluePanda community should be respectful, technical, and collaborative.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in discussions.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on facts and technical clarity.
- Help newcomers with practical guidance.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or hate speech.
- Publishing private information.
- Deliberate disruption of community spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers can edit, remove, or reject content that violates this code.
Repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans.

## Contact

Use repository issues/discussions and mention maintainer `Pato404` for moderation cases.
